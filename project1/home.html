The Issue

Many children in Africa are desperate to go to school and learn how to read and write. But most of the students cannot because they lack funding. With parents already burdened with school fees and parents have to reprioritize the family needs. Most kenyan children have to walk long distances to get to school and usually very early in the morning. Our children trek long distances for a long period of time and by the time they arrive at school, they are exhausted even before the day starts for learning. It is also crucial to understand that they make the same way back home and will get home when it's late. Most parents are skeptical that the funds they give their children will be lost during play or the students decide to use the funds alternatively for snacks or toys.

The Solution

Tap to school is a fintech technology where these children are able to wear wristbands that are equipped with funds that can be used as virtual fares for their commute to school. What they need to do is to flag down one of our select matatu partners, get on board and be transported to the nearest bus stop near their school with a base fare per day. Parents are able to recharge these wristbands once the funds are depleted, depending on the school calendar.


Impact

Students are able to make smooth commutes to and from school

It is safe

Funds are well utilized and not lost

Tap 2 School

Tap4School is a digital mobile platform that uses cutting edge FinTech to enable public primary school children to access school easily and on time. Parents pay for the subsidized fares using mobile money.
